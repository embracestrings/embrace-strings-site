How to Use Your Chinrest:

Chinrest Setup Guide

  1. 1. Attach the Chinrest Clamp Bands
    Place bands around your current chinrest and ensure a secure, even fit.
  2. 2. Position and Secure the Chinrest
    Align with your jawline and tighten gently for stability and comfort.
  3. 3. Set Shoulder and Jaw Balance
    Adjust height and tilt so your posture remains natural while playing.
  4. 4. Fine-Tune for Performance
    Test in short sessions and refine settings for your instrument and technique.
