Frequently Asked Questions

Will this chinrest fit my body type?

Yes! Embrace Strings chinrests are fully adjustable in height and tilt, designed to accommodate players of all sizes, shapes, and playing styles. Whether you're petite or tall, beginner or professional, you can easily find a fit that feels natural and supports your posture.

Do I need tools to adjust it?

No tools are needed. Every adjustment is made with simple side dials, so you can change height or tilt in seconds—even during rehearsal, lessons, or performance. Comfort and posture adjustments are effortless and immediate.

How often should I adjust my Embrace Strings Chinrest?

As often as your body needs! Posture, clothing, and playing style can shift from day to day, or even during practice. Quick, tool-free adjustments make it easy to respond instantly and maintain natural posture every time you play.

Can I adjust it during performance?

Absolutely. The intuitive, side-dial system is designed for effortless adjustments, so you can tweak height or tilt between pieces, without interrupting your flow or needing a tool.

Is it compatible with all violins and violas?

Yes! Embrace Strings chinrests are designed to fit standard violin and viola models. If you have a unique or antique instrument, contact us —we'll help ensure a perfect fit.

Why choose Embrace Strings over other adjustable chinrests?

Unlike other adjustable chinrests that require tools and time-consuming tweaks, Embrace Strings gives you effortless, immediate control over height and tilt. That means you can maintain comfort, posture, and freedom of expression every time you play—without compromise.
